description=[h1]Equipment UI[/h1]
description=Equipment UI is an addition to Project Zomboids interface that adds a paper doll (STALKER / tArKov inspired) equipment panel for managing and viewing your character's equipment.
description=
description=[i][b]Use mod options to hide equipped items from your inventory.[/b][/i]

description=[h3]Drag and Drop[/h3]
description=Drag and Drop items to and from the equipment ui to easily adjust your equipment!
description=Equipment slots will highlight and change color to visualize conflicting equipment.
description=
description=[h3]Customize Your Layout[/h3]
description=The equipment window can be detached and positioned anywhere. It will follow and collapse with the inventory when it is attached. The layout is saved between sessions.
description=
description=[h3]Hotkey[/h3]
description=Includes a customizable control for opening/closing the Equipment UI. The keyboard control can be adjusted in the game's regular control options. The controller binding can be changed via Mod Options.
description=The keyboard defaults to comma.
description=The controller defaults to SELECT (or LS/L3 if using [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2922618524&searchtext=wookie]Wookiee Gamepad Support[/url])
description=
description=
description=[h3]Mod Options[/h3]
description=Install mod options to adjust the mod to your liking!
description=Hide equipped items from your inventory and change the scale of the Equipment UI!
