description=[h3]Drag and Drop[/h3]
description=Drag and Drop items to and from the equipment ui to easily adjust your equipment!
description=Equipment slots will highlight and change color to visualize conflicting equipment.
description=
description=[h3]Customize Your Layout[/h3]
description=The equipment window can be detached and positioned anywhere. It will follow and collapse with the inventory when it is attached. The layout is saved between sessions.
description=
description=[h3]Hotkey[/h3]
description=Includes a customizable hotkey for opening/closing the Equipment UI. It can be adjusted in the game's regular control options.
description=The key defaults to comma.
description=
